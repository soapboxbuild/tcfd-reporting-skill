Draft a TCFD-aligned climate disclosure:

1. Governance: board/management oversight of climate risk
2. Strategy: climate risks and opportunities across short/medium/long term; scenario analysis summary
3. Risk Management: process for identifying, assessing, managing climate risk
4. Metrics and Targets: Scope 1/2/3 emissions, energy intensity, physical risk exposure, climate targets

Format as investor-ready disclosure language suitable for annual report, GRESB, or CDP submission.
