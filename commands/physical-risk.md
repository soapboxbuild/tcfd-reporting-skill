Assess physical climate risk for this real estate portfolio:

1. Map each asset's exposure to acute risks: flooding, wildfire, hurricane, extreme heat
2. Assess chronic risks: sea level rise, water stress, heat stress
3. Score each asset 1-5 on each hazard type
4. Identify highest-risk assets and most material risk types
5. Calculate % of portfolio value/SF in high-risk zones
6. Recommend: immediate actions, monitoring, insurance review

Present as an asset-level heatmap and portfolio summary.
